Humanitarian Assistance to Families with Children
  • Welcome, this page is meant to help UNICEF and its humanitarian partners to register families with children in Ukraine who need humanitarian assistance inside Ukraine as a result of the war.
  • UNICEF encourage all families with children who consider themselves in need to apply for assistance through this service.
  • Participating in this survey does not mean that you are entitled to assistance. UNICEF and humanitarian partners will analyze the data for possible eligibility.
    • Currently UNICEF will assist
    • large families, with three children or more and at least one of them below two years of age.
    • Single headed families with at least two children regardless of age
      • As UNICEF receives more funds from its donors the criteria may be broaden. UNICEF encourages all families with children who consider themselves in need of cash assistance to register.
Upon clicking Consent and Register you agree that your personal data will be stored by UNICEF and will be shared with private sector partners for payment, Ministry of Social Protection of Ukraine and humanitarian partners to avoid multiple assistance streams.
